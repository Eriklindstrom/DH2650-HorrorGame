The House Next Door

ABOUT

An increasingly intensive psychological horror mystery game. An enhanced “escape room” VR experience for the audience that wants to be fully immersed into an adrenaline rush, intense escape-the-house experience. In order to escape and complete the game, the players will have to solve different kinds of puzzles while discovering the hidden secrets of the house and slowly unfolding the story of the game. The game is designed as a VR experience, the goal is that the extra level of immersion will enable the players to test their limits trying to stay cool under pressure. The immersion will also make the exploration of a house a particularly thrilling experience.

WHO WE ARE

We are SPEC-B, Savvas, Pontus, Eric, Constandinos and Bjarni, a team of five from KTH. The game was developed for the purposes of the Computer Game Design course.
